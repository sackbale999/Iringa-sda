About Us

Iringa Seventh-day Adventist Church is a vibrant community of faith committed to sharing the love of Jesus Christ. Located in Iringa, Tanzania, our church has served the community for many years through worship, outreach, and spiritual growth.

Our mission is to proclaim the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ in the context of the Three Angels’ Messages (Revelation 14:6–12), making disciples, and preparing people for the soon return of our Lord.

We invite you to worship with us every Sabbath and join our weekly programs and Bible studies.
